Jonas Brothers - Sucker

Happiness Begins Album

The Jonas Brothers made a heartwarming comeback with their 2019 Happiness Begins album. This is their fifth studio album, released on June 7 by Republic Records. Their comeback single, Sucker, reached number one in several countries. The song's music video features the three brothers' wives and won Best Pop Video at the 2019 MTV Video Music Awards.
